Accordion

UI element made up of vertically stacked items. Each item can be expanded or collapsed to reveal or hide its content.

+ – When should you use an accordion?

Anytime you want to toggle the display of some content.

+ – Why should you use it?

It's great for saving (vertical) space in your emails.

+ – Can I have just one item open at a time?

Sure, just use the expand-single-section attribute on the <amp-accordion> element.

+ – What if I want one of the items already expanded?

Add the expanded attribute on the <section> element you want to show as expanded.
